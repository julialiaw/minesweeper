# minesweeper
this is your typical minesweeper but customized a tad cuter!!

this was my first ever truly polished project, made using html, js, & css, with custom assets drawn in Procreate.

## how to play
minesweeper is all about uncovering the safe squares and avoiding the mines. the numbers on a tile reveal the amount of mines in the surrounding 8 tiles. use your logic and determination to figure out the safe areas.

**right-click** to uncover a tile. its been designed so your first click is always safe! 
**left click** to flag wherever you think the mines are (left click again to remove a flag). you'll need to flag all areas with mines to clear the game.

ready to play again? just press the "new game" button!

## a litle more on the journey
minesweeper has always been a favorite past time of mine, so i figured why not make it? fleshing out the logic behind the game was challenging but fun, especially when i have minimal coding experience. once i laid out what mechanics and functions i needed, finding the right tutorials wasn't too bad!

i took the opportunity to customize everything just a bit! i had fun drawing cute icons from scratch, and adding custom fonts and a cool color scheme. other versions of minesweeper are more retro themed, and although they're pretty cool, my design is slightly more my vibe and slightly cozier n cuter!!
